Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Tai Motion ("we," "us") collects, uses, shares, and protects personal information when You use taimotion.com, app.taimotion.com, and related services (the "Services"). It forms part of, and uses terms defined in, our Terms of Service. If You do not agree, please do not use the Services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account details: name, email address, and password.
  • Quiz / profile information: answers You give in the onboarding quiz and profile — for example age range, gender, height and weight, activity level, goals, and preferences — which You provide to personalize the Services. Some of this may relate to Your general wellness; You choose what to share and it is used only to tailor Your experience.
  • Usage & progress data: workouts, meals, lessons, tracking entries, and how You interact with the Services.
  • Payment information: processed by our third-party payment provider. We receive limited confirmation details (such as plan, status, and last card digits) but do not store Your full card number.
  • Technical data: IP address, device and browser information, and cookie identifiers.
  • Communications: messages You send us (e.g., support requests).

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide, personalize, and improve the Services;
  • To create and manage Your account and process payments and renewals;
  • To send transactional messages (sign-in links, receipts, service notices) and, where permitted, marketing emails You can opt out of;
  • To provide support and respond to inquiries;
  • To protect the Services, prevent fraud and abuse, and ensure security;
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

Where required, our legal bases are: performance of our contract with You; our legitimate interests (e.g., securing and improving the Services); Your consent (e.g., certain cookies/marketing); and compliance with legal obligations.

3. How We Share Information

We do not sell Your personal information. We share it only with:

  • Service providers who help us run the Services — including our hosting/database and authentication provider (Supabase), our email provider (Resend), our payment provider, and analytics tools — under agreements that limit their use of the data;
  • Authorities or third parties where required by law, to enforce our terms, or to protect rights, safety, and security (including fraud investigation);
  • A successor in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

4. Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies and similar technologies that are strictly necessary to operate the Services, and — where You allow it — analytics and marketing cookies to understand usage and measure campaigns. You can manage cookies through your browser and any on-site consent controls.

5. International Transfers, Retention & Security

Your information may be processed in countries other than Your own, including the United States, with appropriate safeguards where required. We keep personal information only as long as needed to provide the Services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements, then delete or anonymize it. We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect Your information, though no method is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

Depending on where You live, You may have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to processing of Your personal information, to data portability, and to withdraw consent. EEA/UK residents may lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. California and other US-state residents may request to know, delete, or correct their information and to opt out of "sale"/"sharing" (we do not sell personal information), without discrimination for exercising these rights. To exercise any right, contact us at hello@taimotion.com; we may need to verify Your identity.

7. Children

The Services are intended only for adults (18+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If You believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

8. Changes & Contact

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time; the latest version published on the System applies. For any privacy question or request, contact us at hello@taimotion.com.
