Subscription & Refund Policy

Effective date: July 5, 2026

This Tai Motion Subscription & Refund Policy (the "Subscription Policy") sets out the rules that apply to Your purchase and use of the Services, and forms part of the Terms of Service (the "Agreement"). Capitalized terms have the meaning given in the Agreement. By purchasing or using the Services, You agree to this Subscription Policy.

1. Subscription Model and Pricing

1.1 Most Services are offered on a subscription basis. When You order, You select a specific subscription plan from the options shown. Options may include different prices, periods, discounts, limited-time offers, and one-time upgrade options. You must review the price, discount conditions, and terms of the plan You choose before ordering.

1.2 By selecting a plan You agree to its terms and pricing. Your chosen plan is clearly identified in Your purchase confirmation (invoice/email) and is visible in the System.

1.3 Initial Phase and Regular Phase. Subscription plans typically include an introductory period at a reduced price (the "Initial Phase"). After the Initial Phase, the regular (full) price applies (the "Regular Phase"). Example: if You choose a plan with a discounted 8-week Initial Phase, You are charged the reduced price for the first 8 weeks, and thereafter the regular price for each following 8-week period until You cancel.

2. Auto-Renewal and Billing

2.1 Automatic renewal. Your subscription automatically renews at the end of each period, at the then-current price (usually the regular price after the Initial Phase), with payment taken from the payment method on file. Renewals continue for the same duration as Your most recent paid period (a 1-week plan renews to a 4-week period), and continue indefinitely until You cancel.

2.2 Billing procedure. You are billed for the next period after the current period ends, unless You cancel beforehand. Example: if You make Your first payment for an 8-week Initial Phase on July 5, the next charge occurs after that 8-week period. The exact day may shift for payment failures or non-working days.

2.3 Failed payments. If a charge fails for reasons on Your side (expired card, insufficient funds), we may automatically reprocess it, up to 6 times within a 2-month period. If it cannot be charged, access may be suspended until resolved; after 2 months a new order may be required.

3. Cancellation

3.1 How to cancel. You may cancel at any time by logging into Your account or by contacting us at hello@taimotion.com. Deleting the app or website from Your device does not cancel Your subscription.

3.2 Notice. To avoid the next charge, You must cancel at least 48 hours before the next service period begins. If You cancel later, You will be charged for the following period, and the cancellation takes effect at the end of that paid period.

3.3 If You cancel during the Initial Phase, Your access continues until the end of the paid period, but no refund is issued for that period.

4.1 No refunds. All fees are non-refundable, including the discounted Initial Phase and any already-paid period, except in the limited cases below.

  • Our fault: the Services were not provided due to our fault;

  • Exceptional discretion: in exclusive, motivated cases where we determine, at our sole discretion, that a refund is reasonable, fair, and necessary.

4.3 To request a refund under 4.2, contact us at hello@taimotion.com and describe the circumstances. Any claim regarding a charge must be raised in writing within 30 days of that charge. We aim to resolve billing concerns directly and fairly; please contact us before initiating a payment dispute or chargeback, so we can help.
