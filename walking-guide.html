1

Begin with Ease

About 11 minutes • Open space or chair nearby • 3 slow rounds

Focus: Walk smaller than you think you need. Complete the four moves as a circuit.

1. Cloud step walk

60 seconds × 3 rounds
  • Step softly side to side.
  • Let the arms float in a rounded shape.

2. Heel-toe flow

60 seconds × 3 rounds
  • Place the heel gently.
  • Roll through the foot.

3. Wave hands walk

60 seconds × 3 rounds
  • Lift hands lightly in front.
  • Wave side to side as you step.

4. Standing pause

30 seconds between rounds
  • Stand evenly on both feet.
  • Relax the arms.
Small steps make the routine repeatable.
2

Balance Confidence

About 12 minutes • Chair or wall nearby • 3 slow rounds

Focus: Use support before you need it. Complete the four moves as a circuit.

1. Golden crane lift

6 each side × 3 rounds • Repeat both sides
  • Shift weight into one foot.
  • Lift the opposite knee slightly.

2. Lotus balance walk

6 each side × 3 rounds • Repeat both sides
  • Lift the heel or foot slightly.
  • Pause for one calm moment.

3. Side glide walk

60 seconds × 3 rounds
  • Step one foot to the side.
  • Bring the other foot in smoothly.

4. Soft finish stance

30 seconds between rounds
  • Feet under hips.
  • Hands relaxed.
Confidence grows from control.
3

Arms & Walking Flow

About 14 minutes • Open space • 3 slow rounds

Focus: Let the arms guide the rhythm, not the speed. Complete the four moves as a circuit.

1. Brush knee step

8 each side × 3 rounds • Repeat both sides
  • Sweep one hand past the knee.
  • Step forward softly.

2. Forward push walk

8 slow steps × 3 rounds
  • Step forward small.
  • Press both palms without locking elbows.

3. Silk reeling step

6 each side × 3 rounds • Repeat both sides
  • Circle the arms gently.
  • Shift weight with the circle.

4. Standing pause

30 seconds between rounds
  • Return to neutral.
  • Soften knees.
Smooth movement feels easier tomorrow.
4

Recovery & Mobility

About 10 minutes • Chair optional • 2 slow rounds

Focus: Make every movement comfortable. Complete the four moves as a circuit.

1. Forward walk

60 seconds × 2 rounds
  • Walk with short strides.
  • Keep head lifted.

2. Horse mane step

6 each side × 2 rounds • Repeat both sides
  • Step gently to the side.
  • Open one hand outward.

3. Shoulder wave

8 rounds × 2 rounds
  • Lift hands softly.
  • Wave side to side.

4. Quiet stance

60 seconds between rounds
  • Stand evenly.
  • Relax the jaw.
Recovery days keep the week moving.
5

Steady Strength

About 15 minutes • Chair or wall nearby • 3 slow rounds

Focus: Stay stacked over your feet. Complete the four moves as a circuit.

1. Golden crane walk

8 each side × 3 rounds • Repeat both sides
  • Shift weight fully.
  • Lift knee to a safe height.

2. Forward push walk

10 steps × 3 rounds
  • Step small.
  • Press palms forward softly.

3. Side glide walk

60 seconds × 3 rounds
  • Step side.
  • Bring feet together quietly.

4. Heel-toe flow

60 seconds × 3 rounds
  • Place heel first.
  • Roll smoothly.
Balance is built one steady rep at a time.
6

Coordination Flow

About 16 minutes • Open space • 3 slow rounds

Focus: Connect foot, hand, and breath. Complete the four moves as a circuit.

1. Cloud step walk

60 seconds × 3 rounds
  • Step lightly across or forward.
  • Float arms outward.

2. Brush knee step

8 each side × 3 rounds • Repeat both sides
  • Shift back slightly.
  • Sweep hand past knee.

3. Silk reeling step

8 circles × 3 rounds
  • Turn torso gently.
  • Circle arms with soft wrists.

4. Wave hands walk

60 seconds × 3 rounds
  • Walk slowly.
  • Wave hands side to side.
Connected movement feels less effortful.
7

Repeatable Tai Chi Walk

About 18 minutes • Open space or chair nearby • 3 slow rounds

Focus: Use the movements you can repeat tomorrow. Complete the four moves as a circuit.

1. Heel-toe flow

60 seconds × 3 rounds
  • Walk heel to toe.
  • Keep steps measured.

2. Cloud step walk

60 seconds × 3 rounds
  • Step softly.
  • Float arms outward.

3. Golden crane lift

6 each side × 3 rounds • Repeat both sides
  • Lift only as high as safe.
  • Pause gently.

4. Forward push walk

10 steps × 3 rounds
  • Press palms softly.
  • Keep elbows unlocked.
This is your go-to 7-day finish flow.
